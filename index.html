Flight Plan 2025

September 2025

Club Launch & First Meeting

Official club formation, member recruitment, and introduction to RC aviation basics. We'll cover safety protocols, club rules, and demonstrate basic flight principles.

October 2025

Build Workshop Series

Hands-on aircraft building workshops where members construct their first trainer aircraft. Learn about aerodynamics, control systems, and proper assembly techniques.

November 2025

First Flight Training

Begin flight training with instructor supervision. Master basic controls, takeoff, landing, and emergency procedures using flight simulators and trainer aircraft.

December 2025

Winter Indoor Flying

Indoor flying sessions with micro aircraft and foam planes. Perfect for maintaining skills during cold weather and learning precision control.

March 2026

Spring Competition Prep

Prepare for regional competitions with advanced flight training, aerobatic maneuvers, and competitive flying techniques.

May 2026

Annual Air Show

Host our first annual air show featuring member aircraft, demonstrations, and public education about RC aviation. Fundraising for club expansion.
